Manifesto

Where do we look when we navigate towards uncharted horizons? We turn to the Northern Star. In the most inspiring and vibrant part of our capital, that star is 49 North. State-of-the-art workplace uncompromising sustainability benchmark, future-proof icon.

For the past twenty-odd years, the ‘southern’ business district was a sensible choice for many renowned employers. Today, more and more organizations see that Amsterdam North’s spaciousness, waterviews and lively atmosphere are the elements that let true pioneers flourish.

49 North is the place their compass leads them to. The building is a captivating capsule of innovation, energy, and symbiosis with its surroundings. With the historical heart of Amsterdam in sight, tenants set sail for the future and discover new worlds.

Outlooks

A view like view others

Room to think, room to grow. That's the philosophy behind the stunning 360° panoramic views from all floors and the flexible open floorplans. Workspaces at 49 North are designed for open minded innovators. Professionals that break down barriers in order to maximize their performance.

Next up: for future generations
For future generations

49 North aims for the BREEAM Excellent Label. The roof is covered with the maximum amount of PV solar panels, awnings deflect any sunlight surplus from the  insulated facades and water crates retain rainwater that can be used for all purposes imaginable.

Next up: giving people the green light
Giving people the green light

With lush greenery surrounding the building and abundant daylight filling every space, we encourage our tenants to thrive. Here, you can watch your plans and ambitions flourish alongside indoor gardens tailored with your chosen flora.

Next up: naturally conscious
Naturally conscious

Walking up to the building we see an array of green landscaping that does justice to the spacious nature of the location. Urban ecologists tuned the trees, lawns, and foliage to the area to enhance biodiversity. Together with the public rooftop parc, these elements are the true façade of 49 North.
